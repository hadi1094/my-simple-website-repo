Origins of Coffee:

Coffee is a popular beverage enjoyed worldwide for its rich aroma and bold flavor. Made from roasted coffee beans, it provides a boost of energy due to its caffeine content.

Varieties of Coffee:

There are various coffee types, including espresso, cappuccino, and latte, each offering a unique taste experience. People often personalize their coffee with milk, sugar, or flavored syrups.

Coffee Culture:

Beyond being a drink, coffee has become a social ritual. Many enjoy gathering at cafes to relax, work, or chat with friends, making coffee a symbol of connection and comfort
